Our Journey

In the year 1989, B.Ravindra Babu, a young and passionate agriculture graduate, with an agricultural family background had a dream to help farmers to improve agriculture yields. He had a firm conviction, that future of agriculture is destined in the seed. To fulfill his passion he built on his family farm in the village of Daliparru, Andhra Pradesh. A small seed production unit for public bred varieties of rice. The seeds of growth were sown and the journey had begun to build one of India’s premier agriculture Company, which would help farmers and also help in contributing sustainably to India’s growing demand for food and nutrition.
